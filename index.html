Contrary to popular belief, Russian is a very useful language. Slava promotes not only the learning of Russian but also the exploration of Russian culture.
 
This is what you have to do to be a full member of slava.

Attend all of the Monthly Meetings

Slava meets once a month on Friday B block. If you have to miss a meeting for any reason you must notify the Slava officers at tjhsstslava@gmail.com 24 hours in advance or else service hour points will be deducted.

Fulfill Your Service Hour Duties

You must complete at least ____ points worth of service hours each semester to remain a part of Slava.

Log Your Hours

Google form is at the bottom of this page.

Be the Chair of your very own Slava event

In order to secure your position as a full-fledged Slava member, you must be the lead chair in your own Slava event. We will discuss and assign events at one of the Slava meetings.
